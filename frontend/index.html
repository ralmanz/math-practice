What they'll learn

A complete algebra foundation

Seven structured units covering the full secondary algebra curriculum, aligned with major international frameworks, for grades 6–9.

📐 International curriculum aligned · Grades 6–9
UTP Admission Prep (PAA)
Targeted preparation for the UTP admission exam.
Designed for fast exam transfer with included sections on:
  • Aritmética: operations, ratios, percentages, patterns, and number theory
  • Álgebra: expressions, equations, functions, and systems
  • Geometría: coordinates, shapes, area, and volume
  • Análisis de datos y probabilidad: statistics, graphs, counting, and probability
Cambridge Lower Secondary
A structured international pathway for grades 6–9 focused on deep conceptual understanding and progression.
Clear stage-by-stage progression with strong emphasis on algebraic fluency, reasoning, and exam readiness.
  • Algebraic Foundations: variables, terms, expressions, and substitution
  • Expanding & Simplifying: distributive property and collecting like terms
  • Factorising: common factors and inverse expansion
  • Linear Equations & Inequalities: one-step, two-step, and both-sides equations
  • Formulae & Rearranging: substitution and changing the subject
  • Sequences: term-to-term rules and nth-term expressions
  • Simultaneous Equations: substitution and elimination
  • Linear Functions & Graphs: coordinate pairs, gradient, and y = mx + c
Common Core (US)
Skills-aligned progression for middle school students with emphasis on problem solving and real-world application.
Focuses on conceptual understanding, procedural skill, and application through context-rich tasks.
  • Grade 6 Expressions: variables, equivalent expressions, and one-step equations
  • Grade 6 Inequalities: notation, number lines, and simple constraints
  • Grade 7 Equations: two-step equations, proportional reasoning, and percent contexts
  • Grade 7 Expressions: expanding, factoring common factors, and mixed signs
  • Grade 8 Functions: input-output rules, linear relationships, and graphs
  • Grade 8 Systems & Modeling: equations in context and graph interpretation
IB MYP Mathematics
Inquiry-driven curriculum that connects algebra to communication, reasoning, and authentic contexts.
Balances technique with conceptual exploration and clear mathematical communication in structured criteria.
  • MYP Year 1: variables, expressions, substitution, and simple equations
  • MYP Year 2: expanding, factorising, inequalities, and nth-term rules
  • MYP Year 3: double brackets, simultaneous equations, and rearranging formulae
  • MYP Year 3–4 Functions: linear graphs, quadratic patterns, domain, and range
  • MYP Year 4 Extension: quadratics, systems, and advanced algebra modeling
